**Cangyu Jin (金藏玉)**  
Email: jincangyu@163.com

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Assistant Professor at the [School of Management, Zhejiang University](http://www.som.zju.edu.cn/). I received my Ph.D. in Agricultural Economics and Management from Zhejiang University in 2022. My research focuses on food supply chain management and food safety risk management, with the aim of improving food safety governance and reducing health risks through data-driven and analytical approaches.

# Education

* Ph.D. in Agricultural Economics and Management, Zhejiang University, 2022

# Research Interests

* Food supply chain management
* Food safety risk management
